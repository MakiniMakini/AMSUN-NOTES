AMSUN Notes ☰
×

This is an Integrated Learning Environment (ILE) for Association of Medical Students of the University of Nairobi (AMSUN). A student can watch Youtube videos and make short notes on the same page!

Usage

It is all simple, copy a youtube link, paste in the input provided under 'new note', click search and your video will display. Click on play and learn, while making notes on the side. You can read, edit, and delete your notes!
